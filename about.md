# About EyeCue

EyeCue is a transformative, multi-model AI agent engineered by **[Aganitha.ai](https://www.aganitha.ai/)**. It is specifically designed to interpret molecular dynamics (MD) trajectories, generating comprehensive audio-visual explanations that make complex chemical and physical phenomena intuitively understandable.

## How It Works

Molecular dynamics simulations generate massive amounts of trajectory data. Analyzing this manually or visually is extremely time-consuming and often requires significant domain expertise. EyeCue bridges this gap by automatically "watching" the simulations and narrating the key events—such as protein folding events, ligand docking dynamics, and conformational transitions.
